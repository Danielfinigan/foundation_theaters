About

Welcome to Foundation Theatres! Located in Center City, Philadelphia, this theatre showcases all of the newest releases on standard, IMAX, and 3D screens, allowing for a variety of viewing experiences! Located minutes from City Hall, this theatre is in prime location for a fun-filled evening, with tons of dining options a short walk away. This site contains links to current showings, upcoming showings, and the ability to purchase tickets. Join us!
